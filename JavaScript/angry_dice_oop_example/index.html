Angry Dice

Angry Dice is a real-time dice rolling microgame.

The object is to be the first to six by rolling your dice in ascending order pairs. Players each have two dice they can roll and they can hold a die (except a 6), if it rolls to something they will need.

  • Round 1 - Die 1 and Die 2
  • Round 2 - Die 3 and Die 4
  • Round 1 - Die 5 and Die 6
  • If you get two Angry Dice you must start at round 1 again.
